About Us

In Case You're Wondering What We're About ...

Plant Based Yummies was founded in 2004 on the love of food and what value it brings to everyday life. As a chef, Mark Gravis was well adversed on different varieties of food. However he saw the lack of foods that focused on more plant and vegan options.

What Sets Us Out From The Rest

100% Organic Products

Each item we use in our delicious meals are guaranteed organic and/or locally sourced.

No Animals Were Harmed

As part of plant based yummies you can be sure everything you eat doesn't come from an animal.

Made With Love

We take pride in the food we make, so we ensure every item is made with care for the special peoeple we serve.
